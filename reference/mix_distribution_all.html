Distributions for MAP Prior, Robust MAP Prior, Likelihood, or Posterior Distribution

Source: R/data_prep.R
mix_distribution_all.Rd

A function that produces samples which display the different distributions

Usage 

mix_distribution_all(
  select_analysis,
  current_trial_data,
  select_dist,
  param_approx,
  robust_map_object,
  post_dist,
  seed
)

Arguments

select_analysis

Incidence proportion or Exposure-adjusted AE rate

current_trial_data

information whether it is a historical (0) or current (1) trial

select_dist

Choice of which should be displayed Robust MAP/MAP/Likelihood/Post

param_approx

parametric approximation

robust_map_object

map prior; mixture distribution with a non-informative component from robust_map()

post_dist

posterior mixture distribution or MCMC samples from posterior_dist()

seed

to reproduce same figures
